Water
By giving the gift of water, you will be helping to build a rain harvesting system and storage tanks in Terrat village. This will allow the children of Namelock to prioritize their education, rather than having to make the 10 hour trek to fetch water.
Education
By giving to education, you will be supporting Namelock by providing tuition, boarding and school supplies essential to the learning of the 70 students currently enrolled in the school.
Operations
By giving to teachers and operations, you will be supporting Namelock by contributing to teachers’ salaries, teachers’ housing, text books, teaching supplies and ongoing school maintenance.
KIPAWA
The Kipawa Line is a grass roots project aiming to offer women a chance to provide for themselves financially.
